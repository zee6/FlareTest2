Search

  • Boolean operators (AND, OR, NEAR, NOT, and parenthesis ( ).
  • Phrase searches (enclose in quotation marks).
  • Partial word searches (minimum three (3) letters).
  • Filtered searches (using the filter in the Search bar.

Mobile and Tablet

If you're looking at this help on a mobile or tablet the menu at the top of the page is replaced by a hamburger menu .at the top right of the page. Click on it to open the slide-in menu. or, if you're using a PC and wish to use the hamburger menu instead of the top menus, just reduce the width of your browser window.
