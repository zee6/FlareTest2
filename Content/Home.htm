Why not make it easy on yourself?

Here are a few tips you can use to ensure you find the information you're looking for with as little pain as possible.

Search
  • Boolean operators (AND, OR, NEAR, NOT, and parenthesis ( ).
  • Phrase searches (enclose in quotation marks).
  • Partial word searches (minimum three (3) letters).
  • Filtered searches (using the in the Search bar).
  • Search results highlighted with colors.
 Mobile and Tablet

If you're looking at this help on a mobile or tablet the menu at the top of the page is replaced by a hamburger menu icon .at the top right of the page. Click on it to open the slide-in menu or, if you're using a PC and wish to use the hamburger menu instead of the top menus, just reduce the width of your browser window.

 Menus

There is a menu spanning the top of this page. All other pages have a context-sensitive menu (on the right) which displays siblings, parents, and children of the current page.

The three small icons at the top right of the content pages allow you to remove colored highlights, print, and close/open drop-downs.

 Glossary

The first instance of any glossary item on any page is highlighted in Olive Bold. Roll-over your mouse (or touch on a mobile or tablet) to see the definition.

 Orientation

Every page has a line of linked breadcrumbs along the top to help you find your way.

 Images

Click on an image to expand the view.
